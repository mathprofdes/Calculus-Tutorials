Camera Options
==============

This menu has just six quick repositions of the camera to view the system down one of the coordinate axes.

Position to Top of X-Axis
-------------------------

This repositions the camera at the top of the x-axis looking down toward the bottom of the x-axis.

Position to Top of Y-Axis
-------------------------

This repositions the camera at the top of the y-axis looking down toward the bottom of the y-axis.

Position to Top of Z-Axis
-------------------------

This repositions the camera at the top of the z-axis looking down toward the bottom of the z-axis.

Position to Bottom of X-Axis
----------------------------

This repositions the camera at the bottom of the x-axis looking up toward the top of the x-axis.

Position to Bottom of Y-Axis
----------------------------

This repositions the camera at the bottom of the y-axis looking up toward the top of the y-axis.

Position to Bottom of Z-Axis
----------------------------

This repositions the camera at the bottom of the z-axis looking up toward the top of the z-axis.
