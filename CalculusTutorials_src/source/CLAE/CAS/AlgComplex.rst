:index:`Complex Numbers`
========================

Complex numbers come up in the output of many of the options and tool in this program, the following options will help working with them.

:index:`Real Part`
------------------

Simply returns the real part of the complex number.

:index:`Imaginary Part`
-----------------------

Simply returns the imaginary part of the complex number.

:index:`Conjugate`
------------------

Simply returns the conjugate of the complex number.

:index:`Modulus`
----------------

Simply returns the modulus (absolute value) of the complex number.

:index:`Argument`
-----------------

Simply returns the argument of the complex number.
